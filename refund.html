Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Our Commitment

At JugaaduCart, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If you're not satisfied, we're here to help with our hassle-free return and replacement policy.

7-Day Return Policy

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for products that meet our return criteria.

Return Window

You have 7 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return

Eligible for Return

Products can be returned if:

  • Product is damaged or defective
  • Wrong product was delivered
  • Product is significantly different from description
  • Product is missing parts or accessories
  • Product arrived in poor packaging condition

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, products must meet the following conditions:

Original Packaging

Product must be in original packaging with all tags intact

Unused Condition

Product must be unused and in the same condition as received

Proof of Purchase

Original invoice or order confirmation required

Complete Set

All accessories and free gifts must be returned

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Products marked as "Non-Returnable" on the product page
  • Intimate wear and personal care products (for hygiene reasons)
  • Products with tampered or missing serial numbers
  • Damaged products due to misuse or mishandling
  • Products without original packaging and accessories

How to Initiate a Return

1

Contact Us

Contact our support team within 7 days of delivery via:

  • WhatsApp: +91 88650 79136
  • Email: jugaaducart@gmail.com
2

Provide Details

Share the following information:

  • Order ID
  • Product name
  • Reason for return
  • Photos of the product (if damaged)
3

Return Approval

Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours and provide return instructions if approved.

4

Ship the Product

Pack the product securely in original packaging and ship it to the address provided by our team.

Return Shipping

Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are borne by the customer unless the product is damaged, defective, or wrong item was delivered.

  • Damaged/Defective Product: We cover return shipping
  • Wrong Product Delivered: We cover return shipping
  • Change of Mind: Customer pays return shipping

Replacement Process

We offer replacement only for damaged or defective products. Refunds are provided only in exceptional cases.

Replacement Timeline

Once we receive your returned product, a replacement will be shipped within 3-5 business days

Refund Policy

Refunds are provided in the following cases:

  • Product is out of stock for replacement
  • Order cancelled by JugaaduCart
  • Duplicate payment made
  • Prepaid amount (for COD bookings)

Refund Timeline

  • COD Prepaid Amount: 5-7 business days to original payment method
  • Online Payments: 7-10 business days to original payment method

Damaged or Defective Products

What to do if you receive a damaged product:

  1. Do not accept the delivery if the package is visibly damaged
  2. If you've already accepted, take photos/videos immediately
  3. Contact us within 48 hours with photos
  4. We'll arrange a free replacement or pickup
Important

Take clear photos of the damaged product and packaging. This helps us process your claim faster.

Exchange Policy

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you want a different size, color, or product, please:

  1. Return the original product (as per return policy)
  2. Place a new order for the desired product

Cancellation Policy

Before Shipment

  • Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement
  • Full refund of prepaid amount (if applicable)
  • Contact us immediately via WhatsApp or email

After Shipment

Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled. You can refuse delivery or initiate a return after receiving the product.

Quality Assurance

All products are quality-checked before dispatch. However, if you receive a defective product, we take full responsibility and will provide a replacement at no additional cost.

Need Help?

Our customer support team is here to assist you with returns and refunds.

WhatsApp Support +91 88650 79136 Available 24/7
Email Support jugaaducart@gmail.com Response within 24 hours

Important Notes

  • Keep your order confirmation email/message safe for reference
  • Inspect products immediately upon delivery
  • Report any issues within 48 hours of delivery
  • Retain original packaging for at least 7 days
  • We reserve the right to refuse returns that don't meet our policy criteria
